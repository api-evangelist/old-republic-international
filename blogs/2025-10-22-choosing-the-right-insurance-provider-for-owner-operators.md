Not all insurance is created equal, especially when you’re running your own rig. The wrong insurance provider can leave you exposed at critical moments, putting both your business and livelihood at risk. For owner-operators, choosing a provider with deep expertise in trucking and a full range of protections is one of the most important business decisions you will make.
