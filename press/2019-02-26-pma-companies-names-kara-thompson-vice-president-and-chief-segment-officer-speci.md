PMA Companies has named Kara Thompson as Vice President and Chief Segment Officer, Specialty Markets Underwriting. As segment leader, Ms. Thompson is responsible for growing PMA’s captive, program and group business. She reports to Drew Jones, Executive Vice President - Insurance Segments and is based in PMA Companies’ Blue Bell Office.
