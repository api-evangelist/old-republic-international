PMA Companies has named Cindy Slubowski as Casualty Product Leader. Ms. Slubowski will serve as a casualty insurance thought leader and help drive the achievement of the company’s objectives, in conjunction with PMA’s insurance underwriting segments. She reports to Geoff Chester, Vice President, Product Management and Development.
