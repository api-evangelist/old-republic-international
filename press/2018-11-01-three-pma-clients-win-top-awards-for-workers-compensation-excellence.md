Given annually by Risk & Insurance magazine since 1994, the Teddy Award honors excellence in workers’ compensation risk management. The award was named for President Theodore “Teddy” Roosevelt, who introduced one of the first pieces of significant workers’ compensation legislation in the U.S. We congratulate our award-winning clients for their achievements and commitment to workers’ compensation excellence.
